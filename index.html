About Us




Corporate Resource Centre

Amity School of Engineering and Technology is one of the premier institutes of Amity University, Uttar Pradesh. The Corporate Resource Centre (CRC) of the institute organizes various events from time to time to bridge the wide gap between industry and academia. The aim of these events is to give an exposure to the students to make them industry ready and to provide an interactive platform.

What we do





What We do

Counselling & Grooming

  • Inviting Industry Experts from various domain
  • Identifying competencies and skills
  • Personality Enhancement
  • Skill development
  • Mock Group Discussions
  • Interviews

Industry Interaction

  • Inviting Industry Experts for guest lecture
  • Developing strong bonds with industry professional through interaction

Placement

  • Visiting Companies after fixing up the appointment for Presentation and inviting Campus Placement
  • Resumes Preparation and formatting
  • Campus Placement Drive
  • Grooming the students to succeed in Interviews

Final Placements

  • Corporate placement presentation
  • Student teams for corporate presentations
  • Host student details on Amity website

Summer Placements & Evaluation

  • Bonafide letters to students for placements
  • Summer training in respective departments
  • Two months summer training in Industry
  • Weekly reporting to CRC and Faculty
  • Interaction with faculty guide

Organize Events

  • Seminars: CRC creates an environment for initiative taking for organizing various events like seminars and workshops.
  • Corporate Meet: Organizing annual corporate meet where all the summer training guides, HR managers / heads, are invited
